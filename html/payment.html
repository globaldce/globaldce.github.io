a payment system

Designed for the Internet, globaldce offer alternatives to payment systems based on legal tender currencies. It make it possible to increase the accessibility of e-commerce in developing countries.

 All transactions are public, with owners and recipients of these transactions identified by addresses.

 globaldce coins cannot easily be counterfeited or spoofed. The encryption protocol is also designed to be highly resilient against most known computer threats, including distributed denial of service attacks.

 Transfer fees sometimes zero and lower than those of payment establishments or those of fund transfer companies.

 Worldwide transfers possible regardless of country.

 Absence of intermediary (payment establishment, payment service intermediary, bank, custodian): the amount credited is taken directly to the reception address.

 Any individual or company can transfer cryptocurrency.

 Storing the cryptocurrency remotely on a server or for downloading to a medium (USB key, for example).

 The total amount of globaldce coins that can be created is capped, making the globaldce cryptocurrency deflationary in essence (the amount of money can theoretically only decrease over time).
