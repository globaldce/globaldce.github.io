Globaldce is censorship resistant open-source decentralized collaboration environment powered by its native cryptocurrency.

 Globaldce is a cryptocurrency and a decentralized collaboration environnment that enables:
  • Permitless innovation - do not require users to have bank accounts to create a decentralized application
  • Self-publish decentralize applications - no need to set up and administrate a web server or host their content on a third-party server
  • Censorship resistant collaboration - files can be accessed as long as at least one copy remains in the peer-to-peer network
  • And much more!

The goal of of the globaldce is to provide a fullstack collaborative environment for decentralized applications.

 While other web3.0 blockchains only provide backend smart contract functionalities and force teams to rely on web2.0 servers for their front end, we believe here that in order to unleash the full potential of web3.0, decentralized applications should not rely on centralized servers. Furthermore, we believe also that a blockchain based decentralized front end environment coupled with smart contract functionalities can bring about a unprecedented level of collaboration.
